# Hide Your Wealth

RimWorld scales raid difficulty with colony wealth. Every statue you carve and every stack of gold you shelve makes the next raid bigger, which means a well-run stockpile is effectively a war crime against yourself. In 2024 I decided the correct response was tax evasion.

Hide Your Wealth is a RimWorld mod that adds hidden storage buildings whose contents are excluded from the colony wealth calculation. It works by patching the vanilla `WealthWatcher` so items inside the hidden furniture simply never show up on the books.

*Are more and more raids after you and your bountiful booty?* Well, fear no more! Our hidden shelves will keep prying eyes away from your precious possessions!

**Features**

- **Hidden Shelf:** Any items placed on it will **NOT** go towards your colony's wealth. Comes in regular and small sizes.
- **Hidden Genebank:** The same trick for your genepacks.
- Mod settings to tune how the hiding behaves, plus compatibility handling for Deep Storage.

It shipped with translations into French, Spanish, Latin American Spanish, and Japanese, because if you are going to help people commit tax fraud you should do it in multiple languages.

This was the first mod where I patched a core game system rather than adding content on top, and it is the direct ancestor of my later, much stranger RimWorld experiments with [ColonyCopilot](https://github.com/Void-n-Null/ColonyCopilot) and [Artificial Rimtelligence](https://github.com/Void-n-Null/Artificial-Rimtelligence).
